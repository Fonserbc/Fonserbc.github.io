About me

1992' Deltebre, Barcelona, Catalunya, Spain. My name is Ferran Bertomeu Castells, also known as fonserbc. I usually live in Barcelona, though I've lived in Stockholm and Taipei.

I studied Computer Science in Facultat d'Informàtica de Barcelona (FIB) of the Universitat Politécnica de Catalunya(UPC) and in Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan (KTH).

I worked at Goo Technologies as developer on their game projects.

I'm a proud member of the student association VGAFIB dedicated to the promotion and introduction of video game development on computer science students on my Faculty. I was secretary for a while there :)

I like music. I like playing music. I can move myself around the keyboard and I actively play the guitar or anything that has strings that can be strummed or plucked. I like reading. I enjoy Terry Pratchett and, when it is possible, George RR Martin.
