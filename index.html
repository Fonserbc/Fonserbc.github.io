About me

My name is Ferran Bertomeu Castells, also known as fonserbc. I'm living in Amsterdam, but I usually live in Barcelona, and I've lived in Stockholm and Taipei.

I make games. I program them most of the time.

If you want to check my games click here.

I studied Computer Science in Facultat d'Informàtica de Barcelona (FIB) of the Universitat Politécnica de Catalunya(UPC) and in Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan (KTH).

I worked at Goo Technologies as developer on their game projects.

I'm part of the student association VGAFIB. I was part of the board for a while.

I like music, I like jazz specially. I like playing music and writing music sometimes. I play the guitar. I like reading. I enjoy Terry Pratchett and, when it is possible, George RR Martin.
