No information about the player or the players device(s) are stored by the app developer.
In some cases, best scores might be saved and uploaded to Google's or Apple's game services, depending on the device's OS, along with the user's Google Play Games' username or iOS Game Center's username repectively.
This information is never stored by the developer and only uploaded if the user's device has set up and agreed to Google's or Apple's terms of use for their respective game services
