Description

The Off-Score project is a music album that you can interact with. The gamified tracks will lead you through different stories, mood and topic, from a piece of paper inside a copy machine which represent the society, to an elephants hunter that struggles with regrettable memories. You’re inside a music album, so of course there’s always music around. Yet they are not just songs but voices inside you to guide you through the adventure. Welcome to the Off-Score Project, and unveil the secrets behind the music.

History

Singer-songwriter YenTing Lo noticed that not all the people listen to the lyrics of a song. When she was planning to record her own music EP in 2017, this problem came to her mind because the lyrics are the main soul of her songs. Having worked and discussed with Ferran, a game developer who's been interested in new ways of combining music and games, they agreed that the interactivity of games can provide listeners with an experience that highlights the lyrics and enhances the storytelling of the songs. After approaching Ferran's co-worker Vanja for the visual art, the Off-Score Project began its journey.

Features

  • 5 tracks with unique gameplay, story and mood
  • Original jazz fusion songs with allegorical lyrics
